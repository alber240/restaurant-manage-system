How to Use QR Codes

Step 1: Print QR Codes

Download and print the QR codes for each table. Print on sticker paper or card stock for durability.

Step 2: Place on Tables

Place the QR code sticker on each table where customers can easily scan it.

Step 3: Customer Scans

Customers scan the QR code with their phone camera to access the menu and place orders.

Benefits of QR Code Ordering:
  • No app installation required
  • Contactless ordering
  • Orders automatically linked to table number
  • Customers can order at their own pace
  • Reduces waitstaff workload
