About Chicha & Chaimo

Our Restaurant

Welcome to Chicha & Chaimo Restaurant and Bar, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality.

Founded in 2010, our restaurant has been serving authentic dishes made from locally-sourced ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our chefs bring years of experience and a passion for creating memorable dining experiences.

Located in the heart of the city, our restaurant offers a cozy yet elegant atmosphere perfect for family dinners, romantic dates, or business meetings. Our carefully curated menu features a blend of traditional favorites and innovative creations, all prepared with the freshest ingredients.

Delivery Service

Enjoy our delicious food in the comfort of your home.

We understand that sometimes you want restaurant-quality food without leaving your home. That's why we offer a comprehensive delivery service covering the entire metropolitan area.

Our delivery team ensures your food arrives hot and fresh, just as if you were dining with us. We use special packaging to maintain temperature and presentation during transit.

Delivery Information
  • Delivery hours: 11:00 AM - 9:30 PM daily
  • Delivery fee: $3.99 within 5 miles, $5.99 beyond

  • Estimated delivery time: 30-45 minutes
